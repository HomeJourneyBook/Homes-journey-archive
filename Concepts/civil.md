# Vanished Civilizations

Vanished Civilizations is the general term used to describe the many races, societies, and cultures that existed before the beginning of the Current Era.

Although most information about them has been lost, the existence of these civilizations is confirmed through surviving artifacts, ancient ruins, translated records, and most importantly — the Gates they left behind.

At present, Travelers continue discovering surviving traces of the Vanished Civilizations across different Worlds, gradually restoring fragments of their history and, through this process, further solidifying the necessary knowledge and experience about the Universe itself.
