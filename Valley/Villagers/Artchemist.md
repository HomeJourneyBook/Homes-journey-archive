# The Artchemist

Artchemist is one of the villagers responsible for the study and preservation of visual records within the Valley.

Most surviving sketches of Schemes of the Path, fragments of symbols, and reconstructed images of distant Worlds are believed to have passed through his workshop at some point.

Unlike ordinary artists, Artchemist is primarily concerned not with decoration, but with the accurate transfer of impressions, forms, and memories onto physical materials.

Very little is known about the origin of his methods, and Artchemist himself rarely speaks about his work outside the workshop.
