# The Arborist

<img src="arborist.png" style="float: right; margin-left: 16px; margin-bottom: 8px; width: 400px;" alt="Arborist">
Arborist is one of the villagers responsible for caring for the trees and growing regions surrounding the Valley.

Most knowledge related to the condition of the forests, roots, and older tea groves is believed to pass through his hands at some point.

Some villagers claim that Arborist is capable of noticing changes in the trees long before they become visible to others.
