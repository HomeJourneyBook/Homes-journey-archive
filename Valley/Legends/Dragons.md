# 3 Чайных Дракона

## Red Dragon

Guardian of the Great Tea Tree, its crimson scales reflecting its noble lineage. As a loyal protector of the sacred valley, it nurtures the tea leaves with its fiery breath. Devoted to the Tea Gods, it ensures the valley's safety with unwavering dedication.
