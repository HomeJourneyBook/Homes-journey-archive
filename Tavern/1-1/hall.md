# Hall of Fame

Alongside the Tavern's collection of Artifacts, a place of equal honor is reserved for the Hall of Fame.

When a Traveler is chosen — or for other reasons becomes one of the Ten participating in the Ritual of Ten Gates — they are granted a name and a place among those who have already completed their Path and made possible the emergence of something unique.
